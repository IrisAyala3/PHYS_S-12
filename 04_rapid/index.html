Class 4: Rapid Prototyping


I chose to build a kinetic sculpture. In research for kitchen fab ideas, I found a site that has 25 mini-maker projects for kids. One of the ideas is to build a wiggling robot, called a wobble-bot, using household materials. To build your very own wobble-bot, you will need cardboard, batteries, battery holder, DC motor 1.5-3V, 5 corks (or materials for legs), hot glue gun, glue, and of course, googly eyes.
